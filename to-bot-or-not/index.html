ChatOps

ChatOps, coined by Github, is a philosophy to bring the work of monitoring and controlling infrastructure and operations from the command line interface and into the chat window, often with the aid of a useful, conversational AI-based bot that can trigger tasks and send error reports from a simple plain English command.
